Champion of the Goron, Divine beast Vah Rudania.

Daruk, was the champion of the race of Gorons, with his courageous attitude and major supporting role- he made lives feel safer under him. He was noted for feeling big-brotherly, and warm to all of which he touched.

Daruks Legacy

Daruk changed lives as mentioned previously, through being a tender caretaker to his people and link, all the while working on his special ability called Daruk's Protection, granting a red crystal barrier for protection.

Daruk, and his family.

Daruks legacy went on to his grandson Yunobo, of which became a champion and inherited his ability to protect. In his life he became fast friends to Link, which had grown fast to be almost familial.

It is said, that Daruk, and Link, were something of Blood brothers, as shown time and time again throughout Zelda games, the Goron Champion and Link, are always close.
