Lets delve deeper into the pond

Deeper into the pond we go, we can see that there is more than one type of duck obviously, there are several! What are some different kinds of Ducks? Lets get in there and find out.

The different duckies

  • Mallard Ducks; the mallard is a wonderful duck with a unique coloring to its head giving it that green-blue look to it. And only males have this glossy green head with the white collars.
  • Canvas backs; these ducks have yet again a unique coloring with red eyes! How spooky. These guys are also known for being North America's largest diving duck.
  • Whistling Ducks; Whistling ducks, which are also known as tree ducks are a subfamily of the duck, which would be Goose and the Swan family. They're most commonly known for their unique high-pitched, three-note whistle instead of a quack! How cool can these guys be?
  • Alabio Duck; The alabio duck is an indonesian breed of domestic ducks, meaning unlike other ducks, they get to have a nice cared for home usually! They happen to be from North Hulu Sungai Regency, and can fly quite well! These guys also are extremely heat tolerant.
